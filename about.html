ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Savory is a boutique firm located in the heart of midtown, 2 blocks south of Bryant Park. At Savory, we have a common thread that resonates through everything we do: PASSION! We are passionate about the creation of our menus. We are passionate about our quality and where our food comes from. We are passionate about our people and the hospitality we show to our clients, our vendors, and each other.

Our stable of culinarians residing at our 39th street commissary represents a melting pot of talent from all over the world; 6 continents and 18 countries. All of our menus are crafted with quality, locally sourced produce, sustainable practices, and inclusive standards. We are known for our love of food, commitment to our clients, and our “can-do” attitude.
